Hawaii's Favorite Breakfast!

Description

In Hawaii, breakfast is a heavy meal. Our favorite breakfast is portuguese sausage, eggs, and rice. You can even find it at McDonald's, it's the "Breakfast Deluxe" and comes with portuguese sausage, spam, or both! If you ask locals what's their favorite breakfast, they will no doubt say, portuguese, sausage, eggs and rice. Come to Hawaii and try it!

Ingredients

  • Portuguese sausage
  • Eggs
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Cook the rice.
  2. While rice is cooking, cut the portuguese sausage into 1/4 inch slices.
  3. When rice is finished cook the sausages over the stove until the edges are slightly burnt.
  4. Whiles sausage is cooking pre-heat another pan to cook the eggs.
  5. Cook eggs to preference.
  6. Remove both eggs and portuguese sausage from pans.
  7. Plate the rice, sausage, and eggs.
  8. Give thanks and enjoy.
